Resources

Science

Implementation plan

This documents gives a detailed overview of the entire project, from choosing the most suitable sampling locations, describing the required permits to conduct the project locally, to detailing the sampling protocols and the data management process. It is the reference guide that helps the sites to successfully implement the project.

Field sampling guide

This document provides the hands-on guidelines on how to use the sampling kits. These instructions are crucial to ensure achieving the best possible sampling results possible.

Sample information sheet

The sample information sheet is part of the sampling protocol. It is used to record all relevant information when carrying out a sampling event.

Science plan template

This template guides the sites participating in eDNA Expeditions 2026-2028 to develop their sampling and monitoring plan. The resulting science plan will be the reference for the project coordination team.

Legal

Collaboration Consent Form

This document is a collaboration consent form between the marine site and the project. It outlines the terms and conditions of the collaboration, including the purpose of the collaboration, the responsibilities of the marine site and the project, and the data sharing and use of the data.

Project Data Policy

This document is the data policy for the project.

Draft Biobanking Agreement

This document is a draft biobanking agreement between the marine site and the biobank, to define the terms of depositing and use of samples. A final version will be developed together with the biobank.

Media & comms toolkit

Photo and film guidelines

Are you taking photos or videos at your sampling site? Read our seven-step guide to learn what images to capture, how to make the possible shots, and how to send us your files.

Model release

All individuals appearing in your photos or videos must have given their consent for their image to be published on eDNA Expeditions channels. Please download our consent form and distribute it to your team members and anyone involved in sampling activities at your site who may appear in photographs or videos. Ensure that each person completes and signs the form before any images are shared. For individuals under the age of majority, the form must be completed and signed by a parent or legal guardian.
